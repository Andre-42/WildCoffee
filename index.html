Welcome to Wild Coffee, Your Campfire Coffee Brewing Experience!

Experience the art of brewing coffee in the serene Swedish outdoors. Join our coffee brewing courses and savor the taste of nature in every cup. Whether you're a coffee enthusiast or a beginner, our expert instructors will guide you through the process.

The Experience Speaks For Itself:

"I had an incredible time at the coffee brewing course. The setting was breathtaking, and the coffee was out of this world!"

John Doe

"I can't believe how much I learned about brewing coffee in the wilderness. The instructors were fantastic!"

Jane Smith

What's Included:

  • Make this experience a pitstop on your hike, come by car or paddle to our shore site cabin.
  • Hands-on coffee brewing experience in the beautiful Swedish outdoors.
  • Expert guidance from our skilled instructors.
  • High-quality coffee beans and brewing equipment provided.
  • Delicious campfire snacks and beverages included.
  • We are weather proof so even in the rain we can enjoy ourselfes.
  • There is a playground and non-caffeinated beverages for the kids.
  • Kids get 75% off if they don't participate in the coffee roasting.
  • Events are available during weekends and holidays from 31/12 till September.
